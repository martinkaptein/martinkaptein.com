# Privacy Policy

## Who are we

Our website address is: https://www.martinkaptein.com/. This is the personal website of Martin Kaptein. 

In case of doubt, complaint or other reason to seek contact with the owner of this aforementioned website the following Email address may be used: martin@martinkaptein.com.

## What personal data we collect and why we collect it

The only data the website owner collects is data collected using Google Analytics.

Also, Cookies and Javascript local storage are used, in order to make this website function properly. However, these elements are stored on your computer, and can easily removed by clearing Cookies, cache and website storage through your Browser settings. Also, these elements are not used to gather any data about you by the website owner.

This website uses Google Analytics. Hence the privacy policy of Google applies. 

However, the user should watch out for embedded content such as YouTube videos, since in that case the privacy policy of the owner of (in this case) YouTube decides their privacy policy. Hence, this is not under the control of the owner of www.martinkaptein.com.

There are hidden things, which can not be controlled by the owner of this website (www.martinkaptein.com), such as logging through external scripts (for web fonts as an example) or logging by their hosting provider. At the moment of writing this provider is Netlify and is not known to collect data without the consent of the user (i.e. you) or the owner of www.martinkaptein.com.

This website uses static hosting, so a big amount of possible vectors to hack the website of www.martinkaptein.com is eliminated. Hence, your data would not get lost as a fault of the owner of this website.

Also, due to static hosting, the owner of www.martinkaptein.com does not collect any data in some hidden ways, as an example through PHP scripts.

Realistically the only danger for your data lies in the provider of analytics software for this website, Google.

### Commenting functionality

Moreover, for the commenting feature we use the services of Disqus. This data, gathered by Disqus may contain information that could be used to identify you.

However, this does make sense, as in certain cases it would be desirable to connact the commenter.

For this feature (Disqus), however, the user should consent separately. Any data is solely collected when the user (i.e. You) decides to comment. This data is not shared with third parties by the owner of the website www.martinkaptein.com.


## Who we share your data with

The data, which is exclusively collected using Google Analytics is only meant to gauge engagement and is not shared with anyone besides the provider of the analytics solution (Google), which makes sense.

No other data is collected by the owner of this website besides the commenting functionality, which is provided by Disqus.

If there should be any questions, please make sure to contact the owner of this website.

Thank you for your attention!
