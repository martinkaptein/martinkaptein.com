# Welcome to the website of Martin Kaptein!

![Martin Kaptein cover picture](/images/martin-kaptein-main-2.jpg)

## Introduction

*Martin Kaptein*

> Martin Kaptein is a musician, tech enthusiast and polyglot from the Netherlands

## Music

Martin Kaptein is a very passionate musician, and pianist. Currently, Martin is studying classical music in the Netherlands and regularly gives concerts on demand and plays in ensembles.

Martin has a deep interest in different kinds of music and [likes to compose music in his free time](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h8szIPK6GpM). At the ArtEz conservatory in Zwolle, Martin is a student of Frank van der Laar.

[Contact Martin](/contact/) for information about his current repertoire and other requests. Also, feel free to take a look at [Martins YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCosUIzMUriRTgg60vh3EwCQ) to hear Martin playing.

Last but not least, Martin has extensive experience of teaching piano playing to children as well as adults.

## Technology

Furthermore, Martin is a huge technology enthusiast and a software programmer. Martin builds stuff in his free time, some of which can be found on [his Github profile](https://github.com/martinkaptein/).

Martin is a huge enthusiast of the technologies that make the world of today possible, and likes to explore as much as possible.

Moreover, in his free time, Martin writes [blog posts on a variety of different topics](blog/), which can be found on this site.

#### Bachelor research 

Martin made an interesting connection between the world of music and the world of information technology, in his choice of his bachelor research topic: **Can J.S. Bach be immitated using machine learning?**

This research has been (very) successfully completed, and Martin is eager to share key parts of his research on this website (which he hopes to do very soon, by the way). 

In general, it can be said very fairly, that Martin likes to dive deeper into toppics, and present them in the form of a written research; but also Martin feels very comfortable with extensive presentations in front of huge groups of people.
